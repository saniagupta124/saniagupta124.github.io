Ethereal Odyssey

A narrative-driven adventure game with puzzle elements

Project Overview

Ethereal Odyssey is an immersive narrative adventure that takes players through a surreal dreamscape where reality and fantasy blend. The game features innovative puzzle mechanics that challenge players to think creatively.

As the protagonist navigates through memories and dreams, players uncover a deeply personal story about loss, acceptance, and moving forward.

Unity C# Narrative Design Puzzle Design

Development Process

The development of Ethereal Odyssey began with extensive research into dream psychology and narrative structures. I created a prototype focusing on the core puzzle mechanics, iterating based on player feedback to ensure the puzzles were challenging yet intuitive.

The visual style evolved from simple concept art to a fully realized aesthetic that blends watercolor-inspired backgrounds with more defined character designs. The sound design incorporates ambient tracks that shift subtly based on player actions and story progression.

Neon Velocity

Fast-paced racing game with futuristic aesthetics

Project Overview

Neon Velocity is a high-octane racing game set in a cyberpunk-inspired future where gravity-defying tracks and customizable vehicles create an adrenaline-pumping experience. The game features a dynamic difficulty system that adapts to player skill.

Players can compete in championship modes or challenge friends in local multiplayer races across a variety of visually stunning environments.

Unreal Engine C++ Physics Programming UI/UX Design

Technical Highlights

Neon Velocity features a custom physics engine that simulates realistic vehicle dynamics while allowing for the futuristic anti-gravity mechanics. The visual effects system uses particle simulations to create the distinctive neon trails and environmental interactions.

The game's AI opponents use machine learning techniques to adapt their racing style based on track conditions and player behavior, creating a consistently challenging experience across multiple playthroughs.

Arcane Defenders

Strategic tower defense with magical elements

Project Overview

Arcane Defenders reimagines the tower defense genre by incorporating spell-crafting mechanics and elemental interactions. Players defend their realm by strategically placing magical towers and casting spells that can combine for powerful effects.

The game features a progression system where players unlock new spell components and tower types as they advance through increasingly challenging levels.

Unity C# Game Design Systems Design

Design Philosophy

Arcane Defenders was designed with the philosophy that strategic depth doesn't have to come at the expense of accessibility. The core gameplay is easy to understand but offers layers of complexity through the spell combination system.

The visual design uses color-coding to help players quickly identify elemental types and potential combinations, while the UI was iteratively refined through user testing to ensure information is presented clearly without overwhelming the player.

My Journey

I'm a passionate game developer and designer currently pursuing my degree in Game Development and Interactive Design at the University of Southern California (USC), Class of 2028. My academic journey is complemented by minors in Computer Programming and AI Applications, reflecting my interest in the technical foundations of game development.

My fascination with games began at an early age, where I was captivated not just by playing them, but understanding how they were created. This curiosity evolved into a dedicated pursuit of knowledge in both the artistic and technical aspects of game development.

I believe that games are a powerful medium for storytelling, problem-solving, and creating meaningful experiences. My goal is to create games that not only entertain but also inspire and challenge players to think differently about the world around them.

My Philosophy

I believe that the most impactful games are those that seamlessly blend technical excellence with artistic vision. My approach to game development is centered around creating experiences that are not only visually stunning and mechanically sound, but also emotionally resonant and intellectually stimulating.

I'm particularly interested in exploring how games can be used as a medium for storytelling that adapts to player choices, creating personalized narratives that feel uniquely meaningful to each player. This interest drives my focus on systems design that supports emergent gameplay and narrative possibilities.

As I continue to grow as a developer, I'm excited to push the boundaries of what games can be and the stories they can tell. I'm always looking for opportunities to collaborate with like-minded creators who share my passion for crafting memorable and meaningful interactive experiences.
